Who Am I

I am Monech Amit Timbang, born on August 20, 1999, from Sitio Little Cebu, Agora Lapasan, Cagayan de Oro City. I graduated with a degree in Management Accounting from PHINMA-COC in 2024 and am currently pursuing Accountancy at Pilgrim Christian College.

What I Do

While others see numbers as just digits, I see them as stories waiting to be told! I worked as a Bookkeeper at Oro Integrated Cooperative, we don’t just work with numbers—we help build dreams, strengthen communities, and empower financial growth. And I’m proud to be part of that mission!

What I Do Best

Brain Teasers

I love challenging my mind with puzzles, riddles, and logic games. Whether it’s solving tricky problems or testing my creativity, I enjoy the thrill of a good mental workout!

LEGO Building

I enjoy piecing together LEGO bricks to create amazing builds. It’s a fun and creative way to bring ideas to life!

Accounting

Keeping the numbers in check. I enjoy crunching numbers, tracking finances, and making sure everything adds up correctly!

What I Dreamed Of

Since I first set foot in college, especially in pursuit of a Bachelor of Science in Accountancy, there has only been one thing on my mind—the three letters that will follow my name: CPA. Failing to graduate in Accountancy at PHINMA-COC was a deep disappointment, and for a while, I blamed myself for not being able to complete my degree. After taking a year to rest and reflect, I found the courage to continue my journey and enrolled at Pilgrim Christian College, reigniting my passion and determination to earn those three letters. May the Lord guide me on this path I have chosen. Monech A. Timbang, CPA (cutieeee!)
